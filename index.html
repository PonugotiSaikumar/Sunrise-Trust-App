The main Goal of the foundation is to look after Orphans and Needy People.

Motivation


"Sunrise Trust " is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) started in 2013 and Registered with 738. Currently we are working with 4 projects:
ORPHANAGE : Providing Food,Shelter and Education to the Orphans who are underprivileged ,as now with 40 kids.

OLDAGE HOME : Giving Shelter and taking care to the Elder People who are uncared in the society.

ANNADATHA : providing Free FOOD which is quality to the needy in the society.

BALIKA VIDHYA VIKAS : providing EDUCATION to the Girl Child in Telangana & Andhra pradesh who are not able to study with the poor Background; as now with 24 girls. Currently our we are doing our Annadatha project in Hyderabad & Bellampalli.

Our Causes

The Sunrise Trust is a non profit organisation and was started with the objective to provide solution to the orphans. This is to be achieved by providing them with free basic school education, healthy home atmosphere, guiding these children towards understanding the need for healthy living and secure a place for them among the rest of the society.

Our Programs

The Orphanage Outreach Program is designed to reach orphans ages 5 to 15. Our team visits orphanages in their area weekly providing the kids with much-needed love and attention, and teaching them about Christ through the building of relationships, music, and fun activities!

DIWALI FESTIVAL

During Diwali, our children decorate the orphanage with beautiful diyas (earthen lamps) and Rangoli (colorful patterns made from colored powder). They also enjoy dressing up in new clothes, exchanging sweets and gifts, and lighting firecrackers.
